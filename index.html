Learn about 3D printing!

3D printing has exploded in recent years. It is now affordable enough for the general public to make use of, and the practical opportunities it offers for makers, designers, and inventors are endless. 3D printing is now being used in all kinds of projects and crafts; however, since it is a new form of technology, it still has yet to be applied to many things.

On this site, you can learn about how 3D printing works, why it's a useful form of fabrication, and how you can get started with it yourself!
